EnglishPod笔记

Lesson 1

  • I’ll be your waiter tonight. May I take your order?

  • I’m still working on it.

  • For you sir, I would recommend …

  • coke and fries：可口可乐和法式薯条（代指快餐）

  • a complimentary glass of wine：一杯附赠的酒

  • I’ll go with … = I’ll choose/take …

  • Your order will be ready soon.

  • grab
    I’ll just go grab a burger across the street.

  • fancy restaurant：高档餐厅

Lesson 2

  • How may I help you?
  • quite ill = very sick
  • sore throat/back/arm…
  • running nose
  • slightly feverish：感觉有点发烧
  • I’m coming down with … (the flu/cold/cough/…)
  • I was hoping to …
  • I’m calling in sick. （打电话请病假）
  • take the day off 请假一天
  • I won’t be able to make it into work today. 今天不能去上班了

Lesson 3

  • What can I do for you?
  • I’d like to check in. 办理入住、登机…手续
    check out：办理结账手续（退房、出院…）
  • I have a reservation under the name …
  • We’ve been expecting you.
  • presidential suite：总统套间
    deluxe room：豪华间
    standard room：标准间
  • There must be some mistakes.
  • Let me double check.
  • There seems to be a mix-up. 弄错了
  • We’re pleased to offer you a complementary upgrade. 免费升级（房间、航班、舱位等）

Lesson 4

  • The timing is just not right.
  • take weight off my shoulders：减轻了我的负担
  • give me a hand with …
  • keep our costs down：减少开支
  • That sounds reasonable.
  • That’s mostly the case. 通常都是这样。

Lesson 5

  • cut in line：插队
    jump the queue
  • golf course：高尔夫球场
  • It must be a mile long. 形容非常长
    （It’s just an exaggeration.）
  • There is no way …
  • Says who? 谁说的？
  • Sue me! 有本事告我啊

Lesson 6

  • road trip
  • The car is packed. 东西都已经在车上了
  • munchies 零食
  • fill up the (gas) tank
  • make a pit stop （stop for a short time during a long journey by road for a rest, meal, gas, etc）
  • We’ve got all our bases covered. （to do everything necessary to be sure that something is successful）所有准备工作都做完了、东西都准备好了（准备就绪）
  • Let’s get going! 出发！
  • So are we all ready to go?
  • Yep, it’s all set. 一切准备就绪
  • freeway = highway 高速
  • highway toll：高速公路收费
  • Let’s get down to business. 让我们谈正事吧
  • a bunch of buddies of mine：我的一群好哥们

Lesson 7

  • computer virus
  • This stupid computer froze again.
  • It’s acting up again. 工作异常
  • It must have a virus or something.
    pick up a virus 染上病毒
  • Can you take a look at my PC?
  • infected file：感染病毒的文件
    corrupt file：损坏的文件
  • browse the Internet
    surf the net
  • web browser
  • Just give me a second.
  • I’ll be right there. 马上到
    I’ll be right up/down/out … 马上上去/下来/出来
  • It truns out that …
  • … be up to date. 是最新的
    up-to-date anti-virus software 最新版的杀毒软件
  • computer tech：电脑修理工
  • How come? 怎么会？为什么？
    How come you got invited and I didn’t?

Lesson 8

  • How’s it going?
  • What are you doing in this neighborhood? Do you live around here?
  • right around here/there/the corner/…：就在…附近
  • in a bit of hurry
    in a rush
  • We should definitely meet up again and continue our discussion.
  • contact details：联系方式
  • slip my mind：一时忘了、没想起
    This is really embarrassing, but your name has just slipped my mind. Can you remind me?
  • It happens to me all the time.
  • be terrible with … 在…方面很糟糕
  • You know what? 你知道吗？

Lesson 9

  • make so much noise
  • It’s so inconsiderate!
  • keep it down：声音小一点
    Do you mind keeping it down?
  • can’t hear a thing
  • not such a big deal：问题不大、小事儿
  • Sorry about that.
  • Someone’s phone is ringing.
  • switch … off：关掉
  • I’m trying to …
  • What’s going on?
    What the heck is going on?

Lesson 10

  • intermediate 中级的
  • sales strategies
  • We’re holding this meeting because we’ve got to do something about …
  • concrete solutions 具体的办法
  • How do you intend to … 你打算怎么…
  • drive sales 拉动销量
  • in the market 市面上
  • match the competitors 与竞争对手持平（到达和竞争对手一样的水平）
    (be as good as or better than other companies in the same industry)
  • What kind of thinking is that? 这想的都是啥呀
  • It'll never fly. 肯定不能成
  • sales promotion 促销活动
    two-for-one offer 买一送一
    job promotion 升职
  • Dammit.
  • … will be here any minute. 马上就到
  • Do you have any ideas yet? 有什么想法吗？
  • We’re (kind of) thinking about …
    That sounds like something we should think about.
    It sounds like something we should consider.
    I kind of like the sound of that.
  • … and stuff like that.
  • in a junior position 职位很低

Lesson 11

  • I’ve got a bad feeling about … 有种不好的感觉（预感）
  • I brought over a housewarming gift. 我带了一份搬家（乔迁）礼物
  • kick … out：赶出去
  • peek into his house 偷看一眼他家
  • creep me out：让我很害怕
  • You scared the heck out of me.
  • I don’t know if you heard, …
    Have you heard …
  • What’s he like?
  • You have to fill me in.
  • You’ll never guess what I saw this morning.
  • delivery truck 快递卡车
  • just in case 以防万一
  • That makes sense. 那讲得通
  • scrap yard 废料场、废品回收站

Lesson 12

  • The house is such a mess!
  • tidy up
  • spotless：干净的
    immaculate
  • mall = shopping center
    I have to pick something up at the mall.
  • do the chores 做家务
    Here’s a list of chores we need to get done.
  • set the table 收拾桌子、摆桌
  • vacuum the rug 用吸尘器清理地毯
  • do the laundry 洗衣服
  • dish detergent 洗洁精
  • sweep and mop the floors
    The furniture needs to be dusted.
  • get all the groceries
    grocery store
    grocery shopping
    grocery list
  • I’m in the middle of something right now.
  • I’ll be there in a second.
  • Why don’t you …
  • Let’s say … 比如说，假设
